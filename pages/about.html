About BFT Capitals

Leading Africa's Bitcoin revolution through education, innovation, and financial empowerment

Our Mission

At BFT Capitals, our mission is to promote early inclusion in the global Bitcoin revolution while building systems that preserve wealth, educate communities, and help Africa rise through financial independence.

We believe that Bitcoin represents more than just a digital currency—it's a pathway to financial sovereignty, economic empowerment, and a more inclusive global financial system.

Our Vision

To position Africa as a global leader in Bitcoin adoption and financial innovation, creating sustainable wealth systems that empower individuals and communities across the continent.

We envision a future where every African has access to Bitcoin education, investment opportunities, and the tools needed to achieve financial independence.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do at BFT Capitals

Education First

We believe knowledge is power. Our commitment to Bitcoin education ensures informed decision-making and sustainable adoption.

Security & Trust

We prioritize the security of our community's investments and maintain the highest standards of transparency and trust.

Community Focus

Our success is measured by the growth and prosperity of our community. We build together, we succeed together.

Innovation

We continuously explore new ways to make Bitcoin more accessible and beneficial for African communities.
